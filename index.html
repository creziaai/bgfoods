About Us

We are a family-owned business based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, dedicated to preserving the authentic flavors of traditional Tamil vegetarian cuisine.

Our products are handcrafted using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. We use only the finest ingredients, with no artificial preservatives or additives.

From tangy pickles to crunchy snacks and aromatic spice powders, each product is made with love and care to bring the authentic taste of Tamil Nadu to your home.
